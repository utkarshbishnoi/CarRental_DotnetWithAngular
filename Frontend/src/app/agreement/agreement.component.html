Agreement Rules

  • Rule 1:Only authorized drivers are allowed to operate the rented vehicle.
  • Rule 2:The car must be returned in the same condition as it was rented, with no damage beyond normal wear and tear.
  • Rule 3:No smoking or pets are allowed in the vehicle.
  • Rule 4:The car should be returned on or before the specified end date.
  • Rule 5:The renter is responsible for any traffic violations or fines during the rental period.
